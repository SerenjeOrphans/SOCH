About SOA

SOA ('Serenje Orphans Appeal') is one of the school charities of Collège du Léman, Geneva, Switzerland (link to school's website). Established in 2009 by Kevin Gilbert (who was then a teacher at the school, but is now retired), SOA now also provides 32% of the annual running costs of SOCH and has been heavily involved in the SOCH Inn and Maize Mill sustainability projects.
