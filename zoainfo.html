About ZOA

ZOA ('Zambian Orphans Appeal') is the main school charity of St George's British International School in Rome, Italy (link to school's website). Established in 2005 by Kevin Gilbert (then, a teacher at the school) as a means to support SOCH through the provision of funds for construction of buildings and annual running costs. The ZOA Teacher Coordinator is John Talbot. A ZOA student committee is elected each year and provides leadership across the campus for the annual fund-raising... Click Here to read more
